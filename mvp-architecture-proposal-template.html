Discovery Questionnaire

Please provide detailed answers to the following questions to help us understand your project requirements and create the perfect solution for your needs.

  1. Application Type & Platform

    What type of application are you looking to build? (Web application, mobile app, desktop software, etc.) Please describe the primary platform(s) and any specific technical requirements or preferences you have.

  2. Target Audience & User Base

    Who is your target audience? Please provide details about your primary users, their demographics, technical proficiency level, and how they will primarily interact with your application. Include information about expected user volume and geographic distribution if relevant.

  3. Problem Statement & Value Proposition

    What specific problem does your application solve? Describe the pain points your target audience currently faces and how your solution will address these challenges. What unique value does your application provide that sets it apart from existing solutions?

  4. Design Preferences & Brand Identity

    Do you have any specific design preferences, brand guidelines, or visual inspirations? Please share any existing brand assets, color schemes, typography preferences, or reference applications that align with your vision. Include any accessibility requirements.

  5. Essential Features & Core Functionality

    What are the essential features that must be included in the MVP? List the core functionalities in order of priority, and describe any specific workflows or user journeys that are critical to your application's success. Include any integration requirements with third-party services.
